Philosophy

Beyond a menu.

The other half is making people feel cared for. That's the part no one talks about — and the part I care about most.

I've always believed the best meals aren't served in restaurants — they're shared around the people you love. My job isn't simply to cook. It's to create evenings your guests will still be talking about long after the last plate is cleared.

My foundation is French technique.
My soul is Brazilian.
The rest is built around you.

How It Works

Six steps.
One unforgettable night.

From the first message to the last dish cleared, everything is handled. Your only job is to enjoy your guests.

01.
Tell me your occasion.

A birthday, a proposal, a Tuesday that deserves better. Every detail begins with a conversation.

02.
I'll design your menu.

A custom menu built around your guests, dietary needs, and the mood of the evening.

03.
I shop.

Sourced from the best local markets and specialty purveyors — nothing on your list.

04.
I cook.

Everything is prepared, plated, and served in your kitchen with fine dining precision.

05.
You stay present.

You're at the table with your people — where you were always supposed to be.

06.
I leave your kitchen spotless.

You go to bed with a clean counter and a full memory of the night.
