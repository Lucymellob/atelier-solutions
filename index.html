About

A Brazilian at heart.
Not so basic, is it?

Born and raised in Brazil, I fell in love with cooking the same way I fell in love with hosting. Through the people, the memories, and the moments made around a table. I trained in professional kitchens and became obsessed with mastering the fundamentals. Knife work. Technique. Timing. Balance. Once I understood the basics, I found the freedom to create something entirely my own.

Philosophy

Every dish
tells a story.

Some carry the memory of my grandmother's kitchen. Others draw from a market in a city I visited last spring, or a technique I fell in love with while staging in Michelin-starred kitchens. My Brazilian soul lives in everything I create. Not always in the ingredients, but in the warmth, the intention, and the way I want people to feel at my table.

Brazilian cuisine has long been underrepresented in the fine dining world. My mission is to change that. One plate at a time.

My foundation is classical French technique.
My soul is Brazilian.
My purpose is bringing people together.
