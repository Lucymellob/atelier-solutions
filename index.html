About

Trained in fine dining.
Built for your home.

Luciana Mello is a Miami-based private chef bringing fine dining technique into intimate settings — from holiday dinners and birthday celebrations to weekly meal services for families who want more than takeout.

Every experience is designed around you — your guests, your space, your occasion. No two dinners are ever the same.

Rooted in Brazilian heritage and trained in classical technique, Lucy blends precision with warmth. Her mission: to give Brazilian cuisine the fine dining recognition it deserves — and to make every table she sets the most memorable seat in the room.

My Mission

Brazilian roots.
French technique.
One plate at a time.

Brazilian cuisine has been left out of the fine dining conversation for too long — and I am on a mission to change that. Every plate I create is part of a larger story: exposing the depth, complexity, and beauty of Brazilian food culture to a world that has barely begun to taste it.

My foundation is classical French technique. My soul is Brazilian. Together they shape a way of cooking that is rigorous and personal, refined and rooted.

Beyond Brazilian

While Brazilian fine dining is my heart, I'm trained across French, Italian, Mediterranean, Latin, and modern American cuisines. Every menu is shaped around your taste, not mine.

Health-Focused Cooking

Beautiful food and nourishing food are the same food. Menus are designed with care for nutrition, ingredient quality, and how you want to feel the next morning.

Dietary Adaptability

Gluten-free, vegan, pescatarian, kosher, keto, allergies and aversions — every menu adapts seamlessly without sacrificing what makes it feel like an occasion.
